Our craft

Designed here, fitted by us

Kitchens are our heart. Every project is drawn by our senior designer Rachael, project-managed by Guy, and installed by our own fitting team — so there's one point of contact from first sketch to first supper.

We work in both worlds: sleek handleless German cabinetry with stone, glass and wood-veneer finishes, and timeless painted Shaker kitchens for period and country homes.

✓Handleless & classic Shaker styles
✓Rotpunkt, Miele, Neff, Bora, Quooker, Corian, Dekton & more
